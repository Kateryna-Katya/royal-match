About the Game

Welcome to Royal Match 3, a beautifully designed puzzle game where you’ll match colorful gems, collect golden stars, and rebuild a magnificent kingdom!
Play through exciting levels, unlock magical boosters, and restore each royal room, from enchanted gardens to the grand ballroom. Combine strategy and speed as you swap tiles and unlock the secrets of the crown.

How to Play

  • Match 3 or More

    Swap gems to create rows or columns of 3 or more.

  • Trigger Power-Ups

    Match 4+ to unlock bombs, color blasters, and line clearers.

  • Complete Objectives

    Clear jelly, collect items, and break blockers to win levels.

  • Earn Stars

    Use stars to restore and decorate royal rooms.

  • Beat Challenges

    Take on special events, timed puzzles, and tricky boss levels.
