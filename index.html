Royal Match 3 – Match, Build, Rule!

Swap gems, earn treasures, and restore the royal kingdom to glory!

About the Game

Welcome to Royal Match 3, a beautifully designed puzzle game where you’ll match colorful gems, collect golden stars, and rebuild a magnificent kingdom!
Play through exciting levels, unlock magical boosters, and restore each royal room, from enchanted gardens to the grand ballroom. Combine strategy and speed as you swap tiles and unlock the secrets of the crown.

  • Solve puzzles to collect resources and renovate the castle

  • Match smartly to trigger powerful combos and chain reactions

  • Unlock new themed rooms and royal structures as you progress

  • Solve puzzles to collect resources and renovate the castle

How to Play

  • Match 3 or More

    Swap gems to create rows or columns of 3 or more.

  • Trigger Power-Ups

    Match 4+ to unlock bombs, color blasters, and line clearers.

  • Complete Objectives

    Clear jelly, collect items, and break blockers to win levels.

  • Earn Stars

    Use stars to restore and decorate royal rooms.

  • Beat Challenges

    Take on special events, timed puzzles, and tricky boss levels.

Game Features

Classic Match-3 Fun: Addictive and satisfying tile-matching mechanics.

Royal Story Progression: Rebuild the palace by completing puzzles and choosing decorations.

Exciting Power-Ups: Use explosive boosters to overcome tough levels.

Daily Bonuses & Rewards: Log in every day for free prizes and spins.

Room-by-Room Restoration: Renovate different sections of the castle with your own style.

Limited-Time Events: Join special puzzle tournaments and earn unique rewards.

High-Quality Graphics: Luxurious design, smooth animations, and immersive sound.

Offline Play Available: Enjoy matching fun anytime, anywhere — no internet required!

Main Characters

  • Queen Isabella – The benevolent ruler whose castle you’ll restore with each win.

  • Sir Puzzleton – The royal handyman who helps guide you through castle repairs.

  • Glimmer the Dove – A magical bird that delivers boosters and bonus gifts.

  • Count Crumble – A mischievous noble who challenges you in special puzzle duels.

  • Lady Flora – The kingdom’s decorator who helps you beautify each room.
